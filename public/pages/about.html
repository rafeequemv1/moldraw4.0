About the Project

MolDraw is a free, browser-based alternative to ChemDraw focused on fast sketching, naming, and exporting of chemical structures with an integrated 3D viewer.

This project is maintained by Scidart Academy as part of our mission to make computational chemistry tools accessible to everyone.
